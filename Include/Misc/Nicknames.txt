Common Nicknames & Their Given Name Equivalents

It is common in many genealogical records, especially more informal records
such as census records and obituaries, to find your ancestors listed under
names you might not expect. In many cases these names may have been the
nicknames that they were known by to their family and friends. Learn which
nicknames may have been used by your ancestors in this list of common
nicknames in genealogy.
